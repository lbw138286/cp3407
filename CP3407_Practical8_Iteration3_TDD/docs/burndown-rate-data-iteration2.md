# Burn Down Rate Data for Iteration 2

The burn down rate graph shows the remaining work during Iteration 2.

## Capacity

```text
3 team members × 4 working days = 12 person-days
```

## Data

| Days Left | Ideal Work Left | Actual Work Left |
|---:|---:|---:|
| 4 | 12 | 12 |
| 3 | 9 | 10 |
| 2 | 6 | 6 |
| 1 | 3 | 2 |
| 0 | 0 | 0 |

## Explanation

The graph starts at 12 person-days because the team capacity for Iteration 2 was 12 person-days. The work left then decreases from 12 to 0 as tasks are completed. The actual work line shows that progress was slightly slower at the beginning, but the team caught up and completed the planned user stories by the end of the iteration.
